The Essential Guide to Handshake Counters

  1. The Snail

    2. Strengths

    • Is one of the most devestating handshakes
    • perfectly counters the Joystick
    • can also be used against fistbumps

    Weaknesses

    • commonly used and loses strength with out a decent rest period
    • extremly weak against The Dodge or The Wrist Wrapper

  2. The Dodge

    3. Strengths

    • When performed on an unsuspecting foe is by far the strongest handshake in exsistance
    • Strongly counters The Snail
    • Instills a sense of dominace and hatred upon the victim

    Weaknesses

    • loses all offensive effectiveness against another Dodge attack
    • Makes you look like a dick
    • can also result in a scratched hand

  3. The Wrist Wrapper

    4. Strengths

    • Easy for beginners to master
    • is decently effective at countering The Snail
    • can also be used in conjunction with a second Wristwrap for a casual bro handshake

    Weaknesses

    • used by beginners so is considered Pussy Shit
    • any expierianced player will notice this coming
    • if you arent quick enough you will miss and look like an idiot
    • Extremely susceptible to The Joystick

  4. The Joystick

    5. Strengths

    • Easy enough to pull off for moderate level shakers
    • perfectly counters the Wrist Wrapper
    • annoys the victim a fair bit
    • can be used while making spaceship noises for maximum damage

    Weaknesses

    • like The Snail can't be used to many times in a row without rest
    • extremly weak against The Dodge or The Snail
    • is also considered simple but not as bad as the Wrist Wrapper

  5. The Rub

    6. Strengths

    • Very Rare
    • can be transitioned into a forarm rub to counter The Wrist Wrapper
    • instills a feeling of disgust and unnerving into the victim

    Weaknesses

    • Can only be used against standard handshakes or on rare occasions The Wrist Wrap
    • extremly weak against The Dodge, The Snail, and the Joystick
